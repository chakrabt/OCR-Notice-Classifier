CIRCULAR NO. 234/2024 - REVISED ATTENDANCE POLICY

Date: 1st September 2024
Ref: ACAD/POLICY/2024/234

To: All Faculty Members, Heads of Departments, and Students

Subject: Implementation of Revised Attendance Policy for Academic Year 2024-25

This is to notify all concerned that the Academic Council, in its meeting 
held on 25th August 2024, has approved the following revisions to the 
student attendance policy, effective from the current academic session:

1. MINIMUM ATTENDANCE REQUIREMENT:
   Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in each course to be 
   eligible to appear in the semester end examinations. This applies to both 
   theory and practical courses.

2. CONDONATION OF ATTENDANCE:
   Students with attendance between 65% and 75% may apply for condonation 
   on medical or other genuine grounds. Applications must be submitted with 
   supporting documents to the Head of Department within 15 days.

3. MONITORING AND REPORTING:
   - Attendance will be recorded for each lecture/practical session
   - Monthly attendance reports will be generated and shared with students
   - Parents/guardians will be notified if attendance falls below 70%

4. CONSEQUENCES OF LOW ATTENDANCE:
   Students with less than 65% attendance will not be permitted to appear 
   in examinations and may be required to repeat the semester.

5. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL:
   Any grievances regarding attendance records should be reported to the 
   respective course instructor within 7 days of the monthly report.

All faculty members are hereby directed to maintain accurate attendance 
records using the university attendance management system. Department heads 
must ensure strict compliance with this policy.

This policy supersedes all previous attendance-related notifications and 
circulars.

For any clarifications, please contact the Dean's Office.

By Order,
Registrar
